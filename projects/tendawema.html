TENDA WEMA Autogarage
A comprehensive branding project for a local auto garage, establishing a strong, trustworthy market presence.

Project Goal

The primary objective was to craft a modern and professional brand identity for Tenda Wema Autogarage. The goal was to build customer trust and position the business for long-term growth by creating a memorable logo and a cohesive set of marketing materials to increase their local presence and attract new clients.

Key Deliverables

  • Custom Logo Design A unique emblem combining automotive and tool imagery.
  • Promotional Poster Eye-catching design for special offers and services.
  • Business Card Professional cards with essential contact information.
  • Social Media Kit Branded assets for a consistent online presence.

Project Impact

The new branding successfully elevated Tenda Wema Autogarage's image, resulting in increased customer inquiries and a stronger, more professional presence in the local automotive service industry. The cohesive design language across all materials has significantly improved brand recognition.
